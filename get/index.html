Advanced — Signers, Verification & More

You'll Need a Nostr Signer

A signer keeps your private key safe and signs events on your behalf. Think of it like a password manager for Nostr.

📱 Mobile

  • iPhone:
  •   Alby Go Signer + Lightning wallet
  •   Nostur Full client with key management
  • Android:
  •   Amber Dedicated signer (recommended)
  •   Amethyst Full client with key management

💻 Desktop Browsers

  • Chrome / Brave:
  •   Soapbox Signer Simple NIP-07 signer
  •   nostr-keyx OS keychain / YubiKey
  • Firefox:
  •   Soapbox Signer Simple NIP-07 signer
  • Safari:
  •   Alby Go Signer + Lightning wallet
